GODAI MENORCA

Godai is an exclusive japanese restaurant located in the 5 stars hotel Suites del Lago, Menorca. It offers the very personal cuisine of the chef Julián Mármol, who owns a Michelín star in his restaurant Yugo the Bunker in Madrid. His vocation is to assemble the Menorca products with the techniques and procedures of the Japanese cuisine and obtain a Michelin star for it too. The restaurant offers several tasting menus of this unique cuisine, described as a “Fusion without confusion” by the chef.

The terrace where Godai is placed is wonderful. Clients can enjoy the views of the Cala’n Bosch Marina, where they can book for boat trips or even rent a boat to enjoy and visit some of the most beautiful coves in the isle (Cala Galdana, Cala Macarella, Cala Turqueta,...).

The restaurant was inaugurated only a few months ago (summer 2021) but it has already reached a high status and popularity. It is known that some celebrities, such as Cristiano Ronaldo, have been tasting this excellent menorquina-japanese food.
The menu and the trip itself might be a bit expensive but for anyone who can afford this experience, it is completely worth it to visit Menorca and try the unique and tasty cuisine offered by Godai.

BURGER & BEYOND LONDON

They’re known for bringing some of the best burgers to the capital, using freshly ground dry aged beef made from choice cuts of meat. They first gained a fanbase trading from a Citroen H van, before opening sites at street food markets Kerb and Street Feast – and finally launching their very own restaurant in E1. Alongside the top chef-quality burgers that gave them their name, they serve American-style sides including Dirty Tots or Fries with bone marrow gravy, plus sauces such as their signature Burnt Butter Mayo, and a drinks list of cocktails, craft beers and wine.

They work closely with Swaledale Foods, who select native breed cattle reared on local farms up in the Yorkshire Dales. The cattle are reared in a totally free-range environment, feeding on grass, hay and silage – resulting in meat that’s succulent, marbled and flavour-packed. Native breeds take longer to mature than commercial breeds of cattle as they don’t grow as quickly, which is what gives the meat its superior flavour.

All of their beef is hung for between 28 and 45 days. They also use Himalayan Salt walls in their drying fridges to create the perfect environment for the dry ageing process. It concentrates the flavour of the meat whilst hanging and purifies the air in the room – allowing ageing for a prolonged length of time without spoilage. This results in a uniquely dry, sweet and flavoursome product.

NUSR-ET DUBAI

Nusr-Et Steakhouse opened its doors in 2010. The restaurant quickly became the sector’s leader with its top quality steaks, unprecedented service quality, boutique concept and still remains as number 1.

By offering many unique options to meatlovers, Nusr-Et quickly brought extra flair to the steakhouse culture and still serves as an indispensable location for its guests.

The restaurant became the one and only address for meatlovers since its opening and continues to be different and special with its rich food offerings, warm atmosphere and top notch service.

Nusr-Et Steakhouse always prioritize its guest comforts and tasty habits in all of its branches and continues to be the number one choice for meatlovers in Istanbul Etiler, Istanbul Sandal Bedesteni, Ankara Kavaklıdere, D-Hotel Maris, Bodrum Yalıkavak Marina, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Doha, Miami, New York And Mykonos.

Nusr-Et Burger carries Nusr-Et Steakhouse’s taste and service legacy into a burger joint concept. Nusr-Et Burger accomodates all of its precious guests in its Bebek, Nişantaşı, Kanyon and İstinyePark branches.

HOSTARIA AL GLADIATORE

Hostaria al Gladiatore is situated in the historic center, right in front of the Coliseum, set in a marvellous location. It offers traditional Italian food prepared conciously and with "love" as your grandma would do. It also have a huge wine selection.

RESTAURANT 58

Located at the first floor of the Eiffel Tower the restaurant 58 offers an elegant design devised by the designer Patrick Jouin. It is perfect either to make a small stop during the visit or to complete your ascension to the top of the Tower. This restaurant will allow you to enjoy the sights from the top.

GINZA KOJYU

Ginza Kojyu is a japanese restaurant owned by the Chef Patron Toru Okuda who opened it in 2003. He named the restaurant after his close friend Nishioka Koju, a renowned Japanese potter, whose exquisite pieces can be admired at the restaurant. The restaurant is situated on the fourth floor of an anonymous building in the Ginza district.

AL'S ITALIAN BEEF

Al's has been the sandwich of Chicago for 82 years, a true food icon, winner of over 150 culinary & critic awards, and has become a destination for people from across the world. It appeared in the documentary "History of food in America" and it is one of the most famous restaurants in Chicago.

SAINT PETER

Saint Peter is an Australian Fish Eatery. They serve sustainable seafood prepared expertly and served simply in a relaxed restaurant environment. In addition, they take the great Australian seafood tradition to previously unexplored heights. Everyday is a new different experience since they frequently change their dishes.

BURGER & BEYOND

Essex-born and London-based, they’re a burger truck turned raved-about restaurant in the heart of Shoreditch. To find the perfect patty, they drew inspiration from burger joints all over the world, from London to Miami, Vegas to LA, Berlin to NYC. So they know a good burger when they bite one.
