GODAI MENORCA

Godai is an exclusive japaneese restaurant located in the 5 stars hotel Suites del Lago, Menorca. It offers the very personal cuisine of the chef Julián Mármol, who owns a Michelín star. His vocation is to assemble the Menorca products with the techniques and procedures of the japanese cuisine

NUSR-ET

Nusr-et restaurants are worldwide known for its spectacular meat preparation, its golden steaks and hamburgers. It has become very popular since lots of well known celebrities have visited them and due to their partcular way of spreading the salt.

DIVERXO

Located in the holtel NH Eurobulding, it is the only three Michelín stars restaurant in Madrid. It offers an fanciful, overflowing and so funny cuisine with evoluting dishes throughout the experience and whose invetion is due to David Muñoz.

HOSTARIA AL GLADIATORE

Hostaria al Gladiatore is situated in the historic center, right in front of the Coliseum, set in a marvellous location. It offers traditional Italian food prepared conciously and with "love" as your grandma would do. It also have a huge wine selection.

RESTAURANT 58

Located at the first floor of the Eiffel Tower the restaurant 58 offers an elegant design devised by the designer Patrick Jouin. It is perfect either to make a small stop during the visit or to complete your ascension to the top of the Tower. This restaurant will allow you to enjoy the sights from the top.
